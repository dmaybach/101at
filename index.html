Accounting

101 Accounting and Taxes’ mission is to provide financial information to clients in a timely and accurate manner, is a commitment that cannot be compromised. Meaningful, well-organized financial records ensure that your business operations will run more efficiently on a daily basis.

Taxes

101 Accounting and Taxes takes a proactive vs. reactive approach to tax services. By keeping current on new tax laws and legislation, I am in a position to identify key tax planning opportunities that minimize both your current and future tax liabilities. 101 Accounting and Taxes provides individual and business clients with the taxation expertise and knowledge that they deserve throughout the year.

Bookkeeping & Billing

Billing is the most important accounting cycle of your business. You want to make sure each job is invoiced and paid in a timely manner.  Get quarterly revenue analysis and recognition. Meaningful, well-organized financial records ensure that your business operations will run more efficiently on a daily basis and are the foundation of a successful business.
